## Threat Model Coverage

Per the plan's `<threat_model>`:

| Threat ID | Mitigation status |
|---|---|
| T-07-01f (ConversationalBot extension subscribes without agency check) | **Mitigated.** ConversationalBot does not subscribe to anything in this plan — it consumes `pendingClarifications` count + handler props passed in by LaunchPage. | T-07-25 (HotspotDot leaks existence of clarification before opening) | **Accepted.** Clarifications are scoped to the operator's own agency via RLS; surfacing them on their own data is by design. |
| T-07-26 (Too many HotspotDots freeze the UI) | **Mitigated by construction.** ClarificationsOverlay groups clarifications by `target_field_path` via a Map and renders ONE dot per field with a count badge + worst-severity color. A field with 50 clarifications still renders one dot. |
